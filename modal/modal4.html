Lash and Brows

Nouveau LVL with Brow Lamination is a popular beauty treatment that enhances the appearance of your eyebrows and lashes. It involves lifting, volumizing, and shaping your brows and lashes to give them a more defined and fuller look. In this treatment, a special solution is used to straighten and set your brow hairs in a particular direction, while lifting and curling your lashes to create a wide-eyed, awake look.

Who its for and how long dies it last

The treatment is a great option for those who are looking to achieve a natural, low-maintenance beauty look. The results typically last between 6-8 weeks, depending on the individual's hair growth cycle.

Looking after your lashes and brows

To ensure the longevity of your treatment, it is important to take proper care of your brows and lashes. Avoid getting them wet for the first 24 hours after the treatment, and avoid using any oily or heavy products on them for the first 48 hours. Additionally, avoid rubbing or touching them excessively, as this can cause the hairs to become misaligned.

Ongoing upkeep

When it comes to maintenance, it is recommended to have the treatment done every 6-8 weeks to maintain the desired look. However, this can vary depending on your individual hair growth cycle.

Overall, Nouveau LVL with Brow Lamination is an excellent treatment for those looking to achieve a low-maintenance, natural beauty look. With proper care and maintenance, you can enjoy the benefits of this treatment for weeks at a time.
