Laser Hair Removal

Laser hair removal is a cosmetic procedure that uses a powerful laser or intense pulsed light (IPL) to remove unwanted hair. This light source heats and destroys hair follicles in the skin, which suppresses hair growth. Using the latest machine and top specifications, this technology banishes all of your unwanted hair forever.

How it works

The light energy is attracted and absorbed by the melanin, which gives the colour of your hair. Once the light is absorbed it is converted into heat which will then heat up the hair follicle structures and suppress the hair growth. Hair must be present in the follicle, in order for the treatment to work and this is why we ask you not to Wax, pluck or thread hairs.

After a free private consultation discovers your personal treatment plan which takes 30-1hr. during which you will be able to comfortably relax. Once complete we will hand over the aftercare for you to follow with your scheduled appointment for your next session.

Multiply treatments are required, this is individually given to clients.

After your treatment

  • The area treated should be kept clean and dry.
  • If the skin feels warm and tender apply a cool compress, Aloe Vera gel or other post-laser preparations.
  • Avoid touching the area treated.
  • No heat treatments for 4-5 days.
  • No hot baths for 4-5 days.
  • No exercise for 24 hr.
  • Do not apply perfumed products to the area for at least 24 hr.
  • No sun beds for the duration of the course and for 2 weeks after.
  • No sun exposure for a minimum of 4 weeks before treatment.
  • Wear SPF 30+ minimum on the treatment area.
  • No self-tanning products for 2 weeks before treatment and 7 days after the treatment.
  • Wear loose clothing to avoid friction.
  • Do not wax, depilate, and no threading on the area.
  • Shave between treatments or cut with scissors.
  • Bleaching creams if used: 7 days after treatment and no later than 2 weeks before the next appointment.

Your results

Results are not immediately visible- IPL might take up to 3 weeks for the hairs to shed (as they still remain in the follicle) - some will be vaporised immediately and some will shed in the following 3 - 7 days.
