Semi-Permenant Eyebrows

Semi Permanent makeup is a micro pigmentation technique which redefines your eyebrows. It revitalises your natural shape using a handheld tool as well as machine method to deposit pigment into the skin.

We offer Microblading, Ombre shading, Combination and Powder brows.

Microblading

A semi-permanent tattoo that creates hair-like strokes with the use of a manual pen containing very small needles.

Combination Brows

A combination of Microblading hair like strokes and, Powder brows creating natural full looking brows with shading darker towards to tails of the brow.

Ombre Brows

Ombre is a semi-permanent powdered brows technique making the tails of the brow darker and fading into a light beginning to the brow.

Powder Brows

Powder brows is a cosmetic semi permanent makeup technique designed to create eyebrows with soft powder effect, very similar to powder makeup.

How it works?

Typically the procedure takes up to two hours with a top-up required after 6-8 weeks. This top-up is to ensure that you are completely satisfied with the brow colour and to add additional hair strokes or/and shading with the machine to further define the previous brow strokes. The procedure is virtually painless and with most, no swelling, but may cause slight reddening of the skin that should disappear in 30 minutes (see our before and aftercare instructions). Suitable for all skin types: oily, thick, sun-damaged skin or dry.

How many sessions will I need?

Typically 2 sessions are enough and the results can last anywhere from 1 to 3 years, depending on different factors and lifestyles. However, a touch-up once a year is recommended to retain the shape and saturation of the pigment.

After your treatment

Immediately after the procedure your colour will appear darker 40% and more intense but will lighten with the healing process. Try to keep out of sun and away from water for at least a week.

Are there any side effects?

Mild redness or swelling may occur for the first couple of days depending on the area treated, but normal activity can be resumed immediately following the procedure.

Your results

The end result will leave you with personalised eyebrows complimenting your face and skin tone, and restore the appearance of realistic-looking brows.

How long does SPMU lasts?

SPMU brows lasts between 1-3 years but requires touch up at least once a year to keep them looking fresh, crisp.
